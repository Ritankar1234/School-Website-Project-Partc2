About Us

South Point School (SPS) or South Point High School (SPHS) is a co-educational private day as well as morning schools located in Kolkata, West Bengal. The school opened in 1954 and introduced higher secondary level in 1960. The school split into two buildings with the high school shifting to Ballygunge Place in 1980. The school is known to have been instrumental in churning out students who achieve eminence in a diverse arena of disciplines and as of 2019, the only school in Kolkata, to have a Nobel Laureate as an alumnus.
